Transcode Downloader

Adds a quality picker to the download action: download the original, or a smaller server-side transcoded version. Transcoding reuses Jellyfin's own encoder (NVENC/QSV/etc.).

Changing this takes effect after a Jellyfin restart.
A transcode is auto-cancelled if its dialog stops polling (closed/abandoned) for this long.
Finished transcodes are removed by a periodic cleanup task after this many days.
Where transcodes are stored temporarily. Leave empty for the default cache folder.
